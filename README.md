## 👤 About

Dev with a background in game development (C++/Unreal Engine 5) who expanded into mobile, AI-powered backend and web. In ~4 years: hardware, networking, a game design degree, and a commercial contract under NDA that shipped on the Epic Games Store.

Today I study Android development while focusing on what drives me most: AI systems and data pipelines.

I'm drawn to problems with well-defined rules that demand elegant solutions.

**Current direction:** AI Engineering — intelligent automation, autonomous agents, systems that reason.
